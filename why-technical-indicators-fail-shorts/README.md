# Why Technical Indicators Fail - Trading Short

## Overview
A 50-second vertical short explaining why most traders lose money using technical indicators, and what actually works instead.

## Narration Style
**Hybrid Approach:**
- DeepSeek-style dramatic hook and storytelling
- Netflix-style clear explanation and structure
- Balanced tone: Critical but educational

## Key Messages
1. 95% of traders lose money using the same indicators
2. Indicators are lagging (built from past prices)
3. False signals are everywhere
4. Crowd behavior creates self-fulfilling prophecies
5. Price action and market structure provide the real edge

## Target Audience
- Retail traders (beginner to intermediate)
- People frustrated with indicator-based strategies
- Those seeking genuine trading education
